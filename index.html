About Me


Patricio Rivera

I am a fourth-year PhD student at the Department of Biomedical Engineering (생체의공학과) in Kyung Hee University (경희대학교). My lab, Bio Imaging Laboratory (바이오영상연구실), studies human motion and action recognition technology using multi-modal sensors and extended intelligence. I am advised by Professor Tae-Seong Kim.
