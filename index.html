About Me


Patricio Rivera

Welcome to my homepage! I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering (생체의공학과) at Kyung Hee University (경희대학교), affiliated with Bio Imaging Laboratory (바이오영상연구실) at KHU. I am advised by Professor Tae-Seong Kim. My research is focused on deep neural models and applying them to re-create images from depth sensors that helps anthropomorphic robotics hands solve real world problems.
